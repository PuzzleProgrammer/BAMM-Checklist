Step 2 - Week 2

Getting to know one another, AND the Hot Mama Business!

Your Week 2 Checklist:

a. Schedule a video call and have your "Meet the OHM" interview!

b. Receive, sign, and send back your Confidentiality Agreement so that we can share more info with you!

c. Learn and study our class descriptions, pricing, schedule example, and budgets.

d. Pass our short quiz about webinar content. Click here for our quiz

e. Meet with Erin, our Business Guru, via video call to answer your questions and talk Hot Mama business

f. Start thinking about your franchise location name and select your population size.

Step 3 - Week 3

It's time for Hot Mama culture and legalese!

Your Week 3 Checklist:

a. Book your Intro to FDD call with Erin to discuss the Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)

b. Sign & send back your receipit of the FDD

c. Suppy a Criminal Record Check

d. Watch your surprise video from the "OHM!"

immerse yourself in the Hot Mama culture...learn, learn, learn!
